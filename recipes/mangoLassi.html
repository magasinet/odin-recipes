Mango Lassi

Description

An Indian yogurt drink - smooth, creamy, and absolutely heavenly!

Ingridients (2 servings)

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 4.5 dl plain yogurt
  • 1 dl white sugar
  • 2 dl ice

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
